## Comparison protocol

1. Compare first fold and hero tension:
   - focal hierarchy
   - composition asymmetry
   - typography voice
2. Compare section rhythm:
   - dense vs sparse transitions
   - repeated container patterns
   - monotony in card grids
3. Compare interaction clarity:
   - visible affordances
   - meaningful hover/focus states
   - motion quality without noise
4. Compare finish quality:
   - spacing consistency
   - contrast/readability
   - mobile polish
