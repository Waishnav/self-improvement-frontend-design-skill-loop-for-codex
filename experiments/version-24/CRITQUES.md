- Action items: Add one hero-state control and one below-fold stateful module tied to visible content change.

### /2

- Strengths: Distinct editorial identity with serif/sans tension, vertical annotation, and convincing paper texture atmosphere.

- Action items: Add one additional transition band (e.g., proof strip or timeline) after current dual-card section.

### /3

- Strengths: Clear blueprint metaphor and disciplined grid language; excellent anti-template deviation from generic SaaS layouts.
- Gaps vs expected: Visual hierarchy can feel uniform due repeated card treatments and narrow tonal range.
- Action items: Introduce one controlled contrast break (color or scale shift) to improve rhythm variation.

### /4

- Strengths: Bold collage posture with rotated card rhythm and energetic headline treatment; strong visual personality.
- Gaps vs expected: Bottom card cluster is text-heavy and interaction-light.
- Action items: Add one media-led or data-led module in lower half plus stateful filtering/toggle behavior.

### /5

- Strengths: Premium monograph style is cohesive, legible, and structurally deeper than prior minimalist routes.
- Gaps vs expected: Mobile stress risk remains due large hero type and long line lengths in key copy blocks.
- Action items: Tighten mobile type/line-length clamps and add explicit mobile-first spacing overrides.
