## Prioritized Issue

- Outputs still drift toward recognizable AI dashboard structure: decorative heroes, repeated rounded panels, KPI-first card strips, blue-leaning dark palettes, and data being subordinated to styling.

## Root-Cause Hypothesis

- The previous skill over-weighted art direction and composition novelty, which is useful for distinctiveness but can push internal UI toward spectacle instead of information clarity.

## Proposed Generic Fix

- Encode a stricter product-UI baseline with a normal shell.
- Use calmer typography and less decorative hierarchy.
- Surface the main dataset earlier.
- Add stronger bans on AI-default chrome.

## Expected Wins To Check

- Main work surface appears earlier.
- Fewer decorative headers and helper blurbs.
- Less repeated card geometry.
- Better table/list/chart selection for the content.
- More believable internal-tool composition on mobile.

## Regression Risks To Watch

- The new rules may become too conservative and reduce visual distinctiveness.
- Tightening the shell and component language may create flatter pages if typography and spacing are not handled carefully.
- Strong anti-pattern bans may accidentally suppress useful expressive moments on landing pages.
